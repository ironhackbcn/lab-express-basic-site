All About Butternut Squash

It may be a winter squash, but the admiration it generates cannot be bound to just one season. Just one cup of cooked butternut squash contains...

  • 7 grams of fiber
  • 52% of Vitamin C (RDI)
  • 17% of Potassium (RDI)
  • 15% of Magnesium (RDI)
  • 457% of Vitamin A!
